# Keyword Research — Colorado Personal Injury (Top 100 Money Keywords)

## Methodology & honesty note
Semrush MCP was connected but its **API units were exhausted** (corporate sub-account — owner must allocate units for a data refresh). Research therefore used: (1) structured PI-vertical demand modeling from well-established search-behavior patterns, (2) manual SERP inspection of head terms, (3) commercial-intent scoring by cluster. **Demand and difficulty are 1–10 relative estimates, not measured volumes.** Re-scoring against live Semrush/GSC data once available is a listed follow-up.

## Selection formula
`opportunity = commercial value × demand ÷ difficulty` — ranked within six clusters chosen for intent diversity (anti-doorway):

| Cluster | Pages | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| geo-casetype (Denver) | 21 | Highest commercial density in the state; firm's home venue |
| state-casetype | 28 | Statewide "colorado + case-type" money terms; less head-term competition than "denver car accident lawyer" |
| metro (other cities) | 20 | Springs/Aurora/FoCo/Boulder/etc. — local intent with materially lower difficulty |
| mountain/ski | 9 | Unique Whiteford wedge: ski/I-70/resort injuries — low competition, high case value |
| value (estimator feeders) | 15 | "settlement calculator / case worth / average settlement" — direct lead-magnet fuel, exactly the demand SB26-174 will orphan when lead-gen marketplaces exit Colorado |
| law (context money terms) | 7 | 2025 cap-change curiosity converts; positions firm as current-law authority |

## Strategic thesis
1. **HB24-1472** (verified: leg.colorado.gov/bills/hb24-1472) raised noneconomic caps to $1.5M (general) / $2.125M (wrongful death) for actions filed on/after 1/1/2025 → every page educates on "the 2025 changes" via one vetted shared component; value-cluster pages capture "what's my case worth" demand honestly.
2. **SB26-174** (verified: leg.colorado.gov/bills/SB26-174) makes third-party legal lead-gen a deceptive trade practice (~Aug 12, 2026) → competitors relying on purchased leads lose supply; owned organic + first-party estimator = the compliant moat. **No paid lead marketplaces are integrated anywhere in this build.**

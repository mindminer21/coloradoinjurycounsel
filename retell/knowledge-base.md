On every substantive intake, collect the caller's actual primary residential address — street and unit, city, state, ZIP — right after name and callback number: "So we can run our conflicts check and have your paperwork ready the moment you finish speaking with the attorney — what's your primary home address?" Collecting it on the first call means conflicts can be cleared and the engagement letter ready immediately after the attorney consultation. Never ask for SSNs, account numbers, or dates of birth.
